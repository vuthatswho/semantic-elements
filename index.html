What are Semantics?

Semantics are descriptive and precise ways for coders to define their items on a webpage. It is better to use descriptive semantics than divs and spans. This is crucial for people who are looking at your HTML code (interoperation). It also helps for internationalization, accessibility, and searchability. Personally, I see a lot of divs and spans in the workplace and it frustrates me. Telling the developers to use clear semantics will help me do my job easier by knowing what to search for when grabbing page elements.
